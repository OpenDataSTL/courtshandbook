Welcome to YourCourts.org!

YourCourts.org helps you understand the municipal in the St. Louis region. Here you can find:

  • Your rights
  • How to prepare for Court (what to bring, how to dress, etc.)
  • Ticket fees
  • Individual court hours, contact information, and websites
  • Case lookup tool (in beta)
  • FAQs
  • and more!

YourCourts.org is a resource provided by CivTech St. Louis. CivTech St. Louis is developing open-source tools to help you navigate the municipal court system. Test some of our prototypes, and join our CivX user testing group.

About CivTech St. Louis:
