Violations Bureau Schedules of Offenses and Fines

Effective April 1, 2015

Below is the standardized schedule of fees adopted by St. Louis County Municipal Courts. Please note that some municipalities have additional citations and corresponding fees not listed here. You may find some of them on the individual courts' pages, listed by municipality on our Courts Information page.

This schedule is divided into several parts:

  1. Court Costs
  2. Part I. Traffic Violation Schedule for Moving Violations (points on your driving record)
  3. Part II. Traffic Violation Schedule for Non-moving Violations

Court Costs

In accordance with Missouri statutes and ordinances adopted by the City, *total Court Costs shall be $24.50. Court costs shall be paid in addition to the citation fine. The $24.50 fee includes:

  • Basic Court Costs of $12.00
  • Crime Victim's Compensation Surcharge of $7.50
  • Law Enforcement Training Fund of $1.00
  • Peace Officer's Standard Training Fund of $2.00
  • Biometric Verification System Surcharge of $2.00

Part I. Traffic Violation Schedule for Moving Violations

These violations can lead to points on your driving record.

Speeding
Up to 14 mph over limit: $6.00 per mile (plus $0.50), plus Court Costs* (See Above)
For 15-20 mph over limit: $7.00 per mile (plus $0.50), plus Court Costs* (See Above)
For 21+ mph over limit: $8.00 per mile (plus $0.50), plus Court Costs* (See Above)
For 30+ mph over limit: Must come to court
